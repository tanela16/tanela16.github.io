Studieau is a creative space — a humble multi-disciplinary design studio with a focus on Art Direction & Digital Design. It is also the creative alter-ego of Ben Martineau. Studieau loves to collaborate with passionate people — combining design thinking, creativity and smart technologies to solve unique problems. Studieau is day by day, pixel by pixel, working with bespoke brands of various sizes and industries to create and grow unique ideas.
